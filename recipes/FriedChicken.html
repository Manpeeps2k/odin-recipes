Description

Different Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps in Making The Chicken

  1. Cut The Chicken Into Parts

    2. Cutting the chicken into different parts such as leg, breast, etc.

  2. Marinate

    3. When marinating chicken make sure to leave it for 6-10 hours to achieve a good marination

    Pat dry the chicken and move on to the next step

  3. Breading Of The Chicken

    4. dredge the chicken inside a flour mixture(flour, cornstarch, baking powder, 1/2 salt and pepper)

    Make sure to dredge it in flour 2 times to have a crispy texture

  4. Cooking of the Chicken

    5. Use a pan and raise it to medium heat, with about 2 inches of oil inside the pan

    Add the chicken in making sure to remove it after a golden brown texture

    We then remove the chicken from the pan and pat the chicken dry of oil
